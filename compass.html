Month Compass

Widerstand — Creation Month (supported by Stabilization)

North Star: Create clarity through one creative decision. Start Signal: Send producer update message → first gentle DaVinci session (30–60 min). Current week: …
Week 1 Week 2 Week 3 Week 4 Not this month

Week 1 — Orientation & Setup

Intent: remove friction, create momentum.

Focus (this week)

  • DaVinci setup + basic workflow test
  • Start / recreate timeline and do a first rough pass
  • Studio appointment (decision micro-project)

Anchors (repeat)

  • Daily yoga 15–30 min + meditation (5 min AM / 10 min PM)
  • Dance workouts: 2× (30–60 min)
  • 1 awareness input (recording or live at 5pm)
  • 1 Life Admin Block (therapy calls/docs) + unwind afterwards
  • Boyfriend: protected weekend block
  • 1 Perspective Trip

Optional (only if energy is good)

  • Face yoga 1–2×
  • Creative collective session (Create or Masterclass)

Motivation placement

  • Watch Minimalism documentary mid-week evening
  • 10 min journaling: “What can be simpler this month?”

Link: theminimalists.com/films

Rule: One block/day max. Minimum session = 30 minutes. Leave a thread: “Next time I’ll …”.

Week 2 — Second Cut Exists

Intent: build the full second cut (imperfect is fine).

Focus (this week)

  • Push the second cut to full-length
  • Light sound experiments on 1 key scene (no perfection)
  • Studio decision made (yes/no/yes-with-conditions)

Anchors (repeat)

  • Daily yoga + meditation
  • Dance workouts: 2×
  • 1 awareness input
  • 2–3 Life Admin Blocks total (across the week)
  • Boyfriend block + Perspective Trip

Optional

  • Face yoga 2–3×
  • Extra review session (watch as viewer, notes only)

Boundary

  • No “catch-up” mindset
  • Stop before exhaustion

Win condition: “A full second cut exists.”

Week 3 — Compare & Decide

Intent: clarity. choose the version you stand behind.

Focus (this week)

  • Compare versions (max 2)
  • Write a short decision note (½–1 page): why this version
  • Test sound direction on key scenes

Anchors (repeat)

  • Daily yoga + meditation
  • Dance workouts: 3–4×
  • 1 awareness input
  • 2–3 Life Admin Blocks total + unwind afterwards
  • Boyfriend block + Perspective Trip

Optional

  • Face yoga 3×
  • Creative collective Create session (ideal for journaling decisions)

Boundary

  • Do not chase perfection — chase conviction

Win condition: “I know which version I stand behind.”

Week 4 — Finalize & Communicate

Intent: close the loop professionally and calmly.

Focus (this week)

  • Finalize chosen version (enough, not perfect)
  • Communicate decision to Producer (Filmwerkstatt)
  • Archive other versions cleanly (nothing is lost)

Anchors (repeat)

  • Daily yoga + meditation
  • Dance workouts: 3–4×
  • 1 awareness input (recording counts)
  • 2–3 Life Admin Blocks total + unwind afterwards
  • Boyfriend block + Perspective Trip

Optional

  • Face yoga 3–4×
  • One celebration ritual (walk, water, favorite café)

Boundary

  • Do not open new major projects this week

Win condition: “Decision made and communicated.”

⏸️ Not This Month (intentionally paused)

  • Major relocation decisions
  • Studio perfection or full move
  • Social over-commitment
  • Catching up on everything
  • Pressure to attend webinars live (recordings fully count)

This list is here to reduce guilt — not to create pressure.

Principle: Focus is selective presence. Stable anchors protect adventure. One month at a time.
