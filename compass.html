Month Compass

Widerstand — Creation Month (supported by Stabilization)

North Star: Create clarity through one creative decision. Start Signal: Send producer update → first gentle DaVinci session. Calendar reference:
Daily Week 1 Week 2 Week 3 Week 4 Not this month

Week 1 — Orientation & Setup

Intent: reduce friction, create momentum.

  • DaVinci setup + workflow test
  • Start / recreate timeline
  • Studio appointment → decision

Week 2 — Second Cut Exists

Intent: finish the full second cut (imperfect is fine).

  • Push second cut to full length
  • Light sound test on one scene

Week 3 — Compare & Decide

Intent: clarity over perfection.

  • Compare versions (max 2)
  • Write short decision note

Week 4 — Communicate & Close

Intent: close the loop calmly and professionally.

  • Finalize chosen version
  • Communicate decision to producer

Not this month (intentionally paused)

  • Major relocation decisions
  • Studio perfection
  • Social over-commitment
  • Catching up on everything
  • Pressure to attend webinars live

Daily Rhythm

One effortful thing per day is enough.

Morning
  • Yoga 15–30 min
  • Meditation 5 min
  • Smoothie / protein shake
Main block
  • Widerstand editing (30 min – 3 h)
  • or Life Admin Block (45–90 min)
After
  • Walk, dance, face yoga, or rest
Evening
  • Awareness input (live or recording)
  • Meditation ≥10 min

Principle: Focus is selective presence. One month at a time.
